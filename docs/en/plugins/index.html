Plugins

Plugins

emotecraft
Enables multiplayer functionality for the emote mod with the same name.
Veinminer-Enchantment
Adds a enchantment called Veinmine that makes a pickaxe break several blocks of ore at once.
AnvilColors
Makes the use of color codes in a anvil possible.
AuthMe
Login system.
BeaconPotionPlugin
Changes behavior of beacons to accept potions as effect input, bringing more effect variety while enhancing immersion.
Clumps
Groups experience orbs together to reduce lag.
CouponCodes
Adds coupon codes that can be redeemed for a reward. Redeem codes with redeem.
CrazyEnchantments
Optional additional enchantments through the ce command. Extremely trimmed down due to overpowered enchantments without enough customization.
DecentHolograms
Uses to make the multiple language hologram in the spawn linking to our website.
DontKillFriends
Makes tamed wolfs less persistent at defending their owner from other players.
ElytraEssentials
Used to nerf elytras to 50km/h and optionally merge an elytra with armor.
Essentials with EssentialsChat
Provides the following commands available to everyone: tpa, reply, mail and suicide.
Eye of Nemesis
Event control system, used to block new features on beta chunks, ensuring those regions remain with beta aesthetic and gameplay. Also used to protect the spawn.
floodgate
Allows bedrock players to join without a Minecraft Java account.
FreedomChat
Disables chat reporting.
Geyser-Spigot
Allows bedrock players to join.
ItemEconomy
Enables diamond as server currency and the command pay, allows tpa and waystone warps to cost diamonds. Your balance can be queried with balance and takes into account your ender chest. (1 diamond = $1)
SkinRestorer
Allows players to change their skins with the skin command.
SweetDreams
Entering a bed in the night time will transport you to a brand new dream world, made of infinite skylands for you to survive.
TintedBeacons
Allows you to supress the beam of beacons by placing a tinted glass on top of the beam.
TimeKeeper
Syncs the game time with the real world America/Sao_Paulo timezone. Phantoms are disabled and their membranes can be crafted with leather, feather and rotten flash.
ViaVersion with ViaRewind, ViaBackwards, ViaAprilFools
Allows players to join on older versions down to 1.7.10, though the player falls infinitely below 0 on versions before Caves and Cliffs.
Voicechat
Enables in-game call functionality.
WaystoneWarps
Adds the ability to create customizable structures that can be discovered by other players then teleported to with a compass for $15.
