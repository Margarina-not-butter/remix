Welcome to RE:mix

RE:mix is a survival multiplayer Minecraft server founded by Margarina. To play with us you have to get on the whitelist, and you can do so by emailing adrianvictor+remix@disroot.org so we can talk about the matter.

The server is composed of several other previous servers of Margarina (most notably the previously public MargarinaCraft server) welded together by chunk transplant.

Gameplay experience is similar to our QuaseVanilla series, consisting of server-side plugins that provide an augmented experience on top of vanilla Minecraft, with all mods being optional. We have an optional official modpack on Modrinth, and please note that the modpack does not follow the server update schedule and may be versions ahead, like when the Tiny Takeover update dropped, there was not enough server-side changes to justify a full server upgrade, thus it comes with ViaFabric so you can change the client version to match the server version on the multiplayer screen.

All gameplay changing plugins are listed below:

ElytraEssentials
Used to nerf elytras to 50km/h and optionally merge an elytra with armor.
BeaconPotionPlugin
Changes behavior of beacons to accept potions as effect input, bringing more effect variety while enhancing immersion.
CrazyEnchantments
Optional additional enchantments through the `/ce` command. Extremely trimmed down due to overpowered enchantments without enough customization.
Eye of Nemesis
Event control system, used to block new features on beta chunks, ensuring those regions remain with beta aesthetic and gameplay. Also used to protect the spawn.
ItemEconomy
Allows `/pay`, `/tpa` and waystone warps to cost diamonds. Your balance can be queried with `/balance` and takes into account your ender chest. (1 diamond = $1)
WaystoneWarps
Adds the ability to create customizable structures that can be discovered by other players then teleported to with a compass for $15.
TimeKeeper
Syncs the game time with the real world `America/Sao_Paulo` timezone. Phantoms are disabled and their membranes can be crafted with leather, feather and rotten flash.
SweetDreams
Since sleeping is disable, entering a bed in the night time will transport you to a brand new dream world, made of infinite skylands for you to survive. Former MargarinaCraft players are probably familiar with this.

Other plugins that are either optional (meaning that you don't need to interact with if you want to) or invisible to normal players can be found in the plugin session of the book.
