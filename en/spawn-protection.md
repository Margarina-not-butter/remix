<h1>Spawn Protection</h1>

<p>The RE:mix spawn is a 14400m² square (120x120) centered at coordinate 0, 0, it's protected from top to bottom of the world. You cannot break nor place blocs, or take books from the lecturn; mobs cannot spawn and endermans don't get angry on eye contact.</p>
<p>Ones restriction will be ignored if it is an authorized spawn editor.</p>
