<h1>Plugins</h1>

<dl>
    <dt>emotecraft</dt>
    <dd>Enables multiplayer functionality for the emote mod with the same name.</dd>
    <dt>Veinminer-Enchantment</dt>
    <dd>Adds a enchantment called Veinmine that makes a pickaxe break several blocks of ore at once.</dd>
    <dt>AnvilColors</dt>
    <dd>Makes the use of color codes in a anvil possible.</dd>
    <dt>AuthMe</dt>
    <dd>Login system.</dd>
    <dt>BeaconPotionPlugin</dt>
    <dd>Changes behavior of beacons to accept potions as effect input, bringing more effect variety while enhancing immersion.</dd>
    <dt>Clumps</dt>
    <dd>Groups experience orbs together to reduce lag.</dd>
    <dt>CouponCodes</dt>
    <dd>Adds coupon codes that can be redeemed for a reward. Redeem codes with <code>redeem</code>.</dd>
    <dt>CrazyEnchantments</dt>
    <dd>Optional additional enchantments through the <code>ce</code> command. Extremely trimmed down due to overpowered enchantments without enough customization.</dd>
    <dt>DecentHolograms</dt>
    <dd>Uses to make the multiple language hologram in the spawn linking to our website.</dd>
    <dt>DontKillFriends</dt>
    <dd>Makes tamed wolfs less persistent at defending their owner from other players.</dd>
    <dt>ElytraEssentials</dt>
    <dd>Used to nerf elytras to 50km/h and optionally merge an elytra with armor.</dd>
    <dt>Essentials with EssentialsChat</dt>
    <dd>Provides the following commands available to everyone: <code>tpa</code>, <code>reply</code>, <code>mail</code> and <code>suicide</code>.</dd>
    <dt>Eye of Nemesis</dt>
    <dd>Event control system, used to block new features on beta chunks, ensuring those regions remain with beta aesthetic and gameplay. Also used to protect the spawn.</dd>
    <dt>floodgate</dt>
    <dd>Allows bedrock players to join without a Minecraft Java account.</dd>
    <dt>FreedomChat</dt>
    <dd>Disables chat reporting.</dd>
    <dt>Geyser-Spigot</dt>
    <dd>Allows bedrock players to join.</dd>
    <dt>ItemEconomy</dt>
    <dd>Enables diamond as server currency and the command <code>pay</code>, allows <code>tpa</code> and waystone warps to cost diamonds. Your balance can be queried with <code>balance</code> and takes into account your ender chest. (1 diamond = $1)</dd>
    <dt>SkinRestorer</dt>
    <dd>Allows players to change their skins with the <code>skin</code> command.</dd>
    <dt>SweetDreams</dt>
    <dd>Entering a bed in the night time will transport you to a brand new dream world, made of infinite skylands for you to survive.</dd>
    <dt>TintedBeacons</dt>
    <dd>Allows you to supress the beam of beacons by placing a tinted glass on top of the beam.</dd>
    <dt>TimeKeeper</dt>
    <dd>Syncs the game time with the real world <code>America/Sao_Paulo</code> timezone. Phantoms are disabled and their membranes can be crafted with leather, feather and rotten flash.</dd>
    <dt>ViaVersion with ViaRewind, ViaBackwards, ViaAprilFools</dt>
    <dd>Allows players to join on older versions down to 1.7.10, though the player falls infinitely below 0 on versions before Caves and Cliffs.</dd>
    <dt>Voicechat</dt>
    <dd>Enables in-game call functionality.</dd>
    <dt>WaystoneWarps</dt>
    <dd>Adds the ability to create customizable structures that can be discovered by other players then teleported to with a compass for $15.</dd>
</dl>
